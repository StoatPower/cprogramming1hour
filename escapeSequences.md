#Sequence               Meaning
* \a                    Bell (alert)
* \b                    Backspace
* \f                    Form feed
* \n                    Newline
* \r                    Carriage return
* \t                    Horizontal tab
* \v                    Vertical tab
* \\                    Backslash
* \?                    Question mark
* \'                    Single quotation
* \"                    Double quotation
